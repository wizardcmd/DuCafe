Drip Brewing TECHNIQUES Coffee

Also known as filter coffee or pour-over, this method involves pouring hot water over ground coffee beans contained in a filter. The water then drips through the coffee grounds, extracting flavor as it passes, and collects in a pot or carafe below. Drip brewing is appreciated for its simplicity and ability to produce a clean, clear cup of coffee with distinct flavors.

Espresso TECHNIQUES Coffee

Espresso is a concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans at high pressure. This method results in a rich, intense flavor and a layer of crema on top. Espresso serves as the foundation for various coffee drinks, including lattes, cappuccinos, and macchiatos, and is prepared using an espresso machine.
